Low and Slow

This weekI am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous
vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vaccum-sealed in
plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to time the target temperature you want
the food to be cooked to. In his book, cooking for Greeks, Jeff Potter describes it as
ultra-low-temperature poaching.
