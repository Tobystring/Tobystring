Low and Slow

Posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week i am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually
vaccum-sealed in a plstic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book.
Cooking for Greeks Jeff Potter describes it as Ultra-low-temperature poaching.

Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before November 30.
